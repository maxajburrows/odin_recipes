BBQ Tofu Sandwich

Description

Need a quick healthy meal? This is hands-down one of the tastiest Tofu sandwiches I’ve ever had! I keep thinking if we still had our restaurant this would for sure be on the menu this week! It’s a fast easy meal, and oh so satisfying!

Thing to Love About This BBQ Tofu Sandwich

  1. It’s super fast!
  2. Simple affordable ingredients.
  3. Over the top in the flavor dept- tangy, subtle heat, smoky, delicious!
  4. Great texture- warm crispy tofu, cool crunchy slaw- a perfect marriage.
  5. The slaw adds nutrients! I’d suggest making a double batch.
  6. It’s vegan!
  7. Will make anyone love tofu. Guaranteed.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Olive oil or coconut oil
  • BBQ sauce
  • Sriracha or red chili paste
  • Cabbage
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Vegan Mayo
  • AC vinegar
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Soft, toasted buns
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Cut the tofu into two slabs, one-inch thick, perhaps trimming to fit the bun. Pat gently with paper towels.
  2. Season a skillet generously with oil, salt and fresh cracked pepper and heat it up.
  3. Sear the tofu on both sides, until deeply golden and crispy.
  4. While the tofu is cooking, make the quick and easy slaw, using precut cabbage if pressed for time. Cilantro and onion add great flavor here.
  5. Mix BBQ Sauce and red chili paste (or sriracha) in a bowl and brush it liberally on the tofu, getting the bottom too. Let the sauce caramelize just slightly in the pan, before turning the heat off.
  6. Toast the buns and assemble! Feel free to add avocado if you like- a bit messier, but oh so tasty! And voila! You’ll have a delicious meal on the table in under 30 minutes! This recipe makes two hearty sandwiches.
